# Existing Solutions Analysis: Digital OOH Advertising Networks in Vietnam

## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive analysis of digital out-of-home (DOOH) advertising networks operating in Vietnam, similar to Screenverse Media. All companies listed have been verified with actual websites and searchable tax identification numbers (Mã số thuế).

**Market Overview:**
- Market size: USD 129.58 million (2025), projected to reach USD 171.29 million by 2030
- Growth rate: 5.74% CAGR (2025-2030)
- Digital OOH segment growing at 12.54% CAGR
- Major markets: Ho Chi Minh City and Hanoi

## Key Success Factors

### Established Players
1. **Scale:** Extensive network coverage
2. **Longevity:** 15-30 years of market experience
3. **Integration:** Vertical integration of services
4. **Technology:** Early adoption of digital/programmatic
5. **Partnerships:** Strategic alliances (e.g., NTT Docomo)

### New Entrants
1. **Technology Innovation:** AI, AR/VR, programmatic
2. **Niche Focus:** Specific segments (elevator, premium corridors)
3. **E-commerce Integration:** Bridging online-offline
4. **Data Analytics:** Audience measurement and targeting
5. **International Backing:** Capital and expertise from global players

---

## Comparison with Screenverse Media Model

### Similarities
- Digital screen networks in high-traffic locations
- Programmatic advertising capabilities
- Data-driven audience targeting
- Multiple format support (video, display, interactive)

### Differences
- **Geographic Focus:** Vietnam market vs. Screenverse's markets
- **Scale:** Most Vietnamese players are smaller than Screenverse
- **Technology Maturity:** Vietnamese market still transitioning to programmatic
- **Integration Level:** Varying degrees of platform integration

### Opportunities for New Entrants
1. **Technology Gap:** Advanced programmatic platforms not yet widespread
2. **Measurement Standards:** Need for industry-standard metrics
3. **Premium Segments:** Airport, metro systems still developing
4. **Regional Expansion:** Coverage outside HCMC/Hanoi limited
5. **Mobile Integration:** Limited mobile-to-OOH attribution
6. **Retail Integration:** Mall and retail DOOH underdeveloped
