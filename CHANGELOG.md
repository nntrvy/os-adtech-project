### Added

### Changed
- `CLAUDE.md` - Added new research documents, key validated insights
- `README.md` - Updated next phase with Xanh SM partnership model, revenue projections

### Validated
- **Regulatory**: In-car screens are legal (Article 32, Advertising Law 2012)
- **Unit Economics**: Break-even at 7% fill rate; $3.78M/year profit at 40% fill
- **Xanh SM Partnership**: 70% ROI, 14-month payback on $6.12M investment

### Invalidated (DO NOT REPEAT)
- Partnering with struggling taxi companies (Mai Linh example)
- Funding hardware yourself (iDOOH burned $3.88M)
- Starting with 5,000 screens before proving model
- Agency-only sales without self-serve platform
